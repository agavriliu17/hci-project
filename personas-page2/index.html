Detailed Description

"A persona is a user archetype you can use to help guide decisions about product features, navigation, interactions, and even visual design." (Kim Goodwin, Cooper.com)

Sandra is a marketing manager at an insurance company, responsible for various marketing activities. As a dedicated mother of two kids, her family's well-being is of utmost importance to her. Sandra values quality products and services that provide a great experience for herself, her children, and her partner. She seeks convenience and time-saving options in her busy schedule and relies on recommendations from her trusted social connections to make informed purchasing decisions. Sandra takes into account the opinions and experiences of her friends and peers when considering different products, ensuring the best choices for her family.

Scenarios

Scenario 1: Sandra is using the interactive grocery recommendation application to find affordable and quality groceries for her family. However, the application has outdated product information and unreliable recommendations. As a result, Sandra ends up purchasing groceries that do not meet her expectations in terms of quality, pricing, and dietary restrictions. She feels frustrated and disappointed with the app's performance, as it fails to provide accurate and helpful recommendations.
End point: Sandra is disappointed and she needs to find an alternative application to help her find the right products for her family.


Scenario 2: Sandra is using the interactive grocery recommendation application to find the best groceries for her family. The application provides her with personalized recommendations based on her preferences, including dietary restrictions and budget constraints. It offers detailed product information, including pricing, quality ratings, and user reviews. Sandra also benefits from the social connections feature, which allows her to see what products her friends have liked and recommended. With the app's assistance, Sandra confidently selects high-quality and affordable groceries that meet her family's needs.
End point: She feels satisfied with the app's recommendations, as they have helped her save time, make informed decisions, and ensure a delightful grocery shopping experience.

Detailed Description

"A persona is a user archetype you can use to help guide decisions about product features, navigation, interactions, and even visual design." (Kim Goodwin, Cooper.com)

John is a software engineer who leads an active and health-conscious lifestyle while managing his diabetes. With a keen focus on maintaining his well-being, John understands the importance of making informed dietary choices. He seeks an interactive application that can assist him in navigating the complexities of grocery shopping, particularly with regard to product labels and information.

Scenarios

Scenario 1: Without the assistance of the interactive application, John faces challenges during his grocery shopping experience. He visits the store and is unable to find clear and comprehensive product information on the labels. The absence of detailed ingredient lists, allergen warnings, and nutritional values leaves John uncertain about the suitability of the products for his dietary needs. End point: John leaves the store without purchasing the groceries he needs, resulting in added stress and potentially compromising his health management efforts.


Scenario 2: John utilizes the interactive application to aid him in his grocery shopping journey. He opens the app on his mobile phone and navigates through the user-friendly interface, searching for specific products tailored to his dietary needs. The app provides him with detailed product information, including ingredient lists, allergen warnings, and nutritional values. John feels confident in his selections as he compares various products, taking into account certifications and quality indicators. End point: With the app's assistance, John successfully completes his grocery shopping, ensuring he has a well-rounded and diabetes-friendly selection of items to support his health goals.
